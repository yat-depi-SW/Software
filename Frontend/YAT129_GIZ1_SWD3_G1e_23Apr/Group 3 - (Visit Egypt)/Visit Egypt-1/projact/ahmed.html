Egypt's history extends for about 7000 years of civilization and progress, starting with the Pharaonic civilization, then the Coptic civilization, then with the Islamic conquest, Egypt contains beautiful and great landmarks such as the pyramids and museums containing antiquities from both the Roman era and the Coptic era in Alexandria and in all governorates of Egypt. The city of Luxor alone contains a third of the world's antiquities. Now, Egypt has the largest museums in the world, the Grand Egyptian Museum and the Egyptian Civilization Museum. There are also some natural landmarks in Egypt, such as the one in Siwa and Sinai. There are also many beautiful activities in Egypt with breathtaking views in Sharm El-Sheikh, Marsa Matrouh and the Red Sea.
