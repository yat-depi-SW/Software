Hotels in Alexandria

Four Seasonsa at San Stefano

If you are looking for a fantastic experience, Four Seasons Hotel Alexandria At San Stefano is a great option. It is a beachfront hotel with a private beach area, so you can unwind on a lounge chair or dip your toes into the sea in a relatively quiet atmosphere. When you are not chilling on the beach, there is a wide array of amenities to enjoy here. There are four swimming pools and nine restaurants and lounges on-site. The two-storey spa, moreover, offers a sauna and a squash court

Golden Jewel Hotel

Golden Jewel Hotel is a 5-star hotel located right on Cleopatra Beach. There is even a nice private beach area, so you can avoid the crowds of a public beach. And if you want to enjoy views of the sea but prefer a pool, there is an outdoor pool that looks onto the beach. Each morning, you can enjoy a continental breakfast. The hotel also has a shared lounge, and free Wi-Fi is available.

Hilton Alexandria Corniche

Based in the heart of the city, Hilton Alexandria Corniche offers its guests a beachfront experience with access to a private beach. You can spend your days taking a dip in the gorgeous blue waters or kick back under an umbrella. The pool area also offers panoramic views out to the sea.

Hotels in Sharm El Sheikh

The Four Seasons Sharm el-Sheikh

The Four Seasons Sharm el-Sheikh is an exclusive hideaway, inspired by traditional Arabian architecture and set amid palm tree gardens that roll down to a stretch of private white-sand beach. Accents of arabesque architecture and interior design continue within the elegant and roomsexpansive, suites, chalets, and villas. All are designed to make the most of indoor-outdoor living, with large balconies or terraces.

Novotel Sharm El Sheikh

Situated in Sharm El-Sheikh, this hotel is located within Naama Bay. It has a private beach, 5 swimming pools, and offers air-conditioned rooms with satellite TV. Rooms at the Novotel Sharm El-Sheikh have tiled floors and elegant furnishings. They come equipped with a minibar, as well as tea and coffee making facilities. Some rooms feature balconies with panoramic views.

Hotels in Aswan

Movenpick Aswan

Located on Elephantine Island in the middle of the River Nile, Movenpick Resort Aswan is a world of green in harmony with nature. A true haven of comfort and relaxation for those who want the best in a quiet and peaceful place. You can stroll between the state-of-the-art birdwatching station , organic farm , deer house , fish pond and more than 3000 fruit trees

Kato Dool Wellness Resort

Live like a local Nubian in an eco-friendly wellness sanctuary. Katodool Wellness is ideally located on the hillside of the Nubian Village in Aswan, embracing the beauty of the surrounding nature of the river Nile and the protectorate of Salouga and Ghazal, preserving the genuine Nubian lifestyle and its mindfulness activities. The resort features 32 Nile View rooms well-appointed for regeneration, distinctively inspired by the Nubian grace. Each room tells a tale of a legend in ancient Egypt history in remembrance of their eternal nobility. All rooms have an exquisite Nile view, are furnished with organic and ecological fabrics for a regenerating sleep, and are decorated with handmade art crafts from natural materials, transmitting the tranquillity of ancient Egypt
